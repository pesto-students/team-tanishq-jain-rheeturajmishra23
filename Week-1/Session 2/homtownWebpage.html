Kolkata, India

Location

Kolkata is located on the eastern bank of the Hooghly River in West Bengal, India.

About

Weather

Kolkata experiences a tropical wet-and-dry climate with warm temperatures throughout the year.

Population

Around 4.5 million people call Kolkata home, making it one of the most populous cities in India.

Sports

Cricket and football are popular sports in Kolkata, with passionate fans supporting local teams.

Things To Do

  • Visit the iconic Victoria Memorial
  • Explore the vibrant local markets, such as New Market and College Street
  • Indulge in delicious Bengali cuisine, including mouth-watering sweets like Rosogolla and Sandesh
